    • The APARA Project

    Defending financial literacy and affordable higher education for all.


    APARA in Romanian means "defend". This refers to our group's goal to defend financial literacy.
    As dual-enrollment students, we understand how important it is for everyone, regardless of
    socioeconomic status, to receive a good education.Part of that means educating students about
    what the financial aid process looks like and how to make college more affordable.

    This website consists of an in-depth college costs calculator and helpful information on financial aid.

    What makes financial literacy
    so important?

    Did you know that. . .


    The average cost of college increases annually by 6.8%.1


    Between 2018-2019, the cost of undergraduate tuition,
    fees, room, and board rose 28% at public institutions and
    19% at private nonprofit institutions.2


    Undergraduate enrollment decreased nationwide by 6.6% in 2021.3
